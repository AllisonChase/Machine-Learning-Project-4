Our Mission

We went into project one looking for a way to assist the elderly in finding solid and reliable nursing homes with high ratings. Through that data we were able to manually make correlations about what to look for in a nursing home. With this next project we wanted to expand on the data analysis with machine learning. Through predictive tools we hope to be able to find even more correlations at higher accuracy rates.
